WE ARE a family owned and operated bee keeping apiary in the Blue Ridge mountains of Virginia. Since 2009 we have been gradually increasing our size, and we now operate three seperate locations. We produce and sell honey, bees wax candles, queens, and nucs.

OUR MISSION is to sustainably increase the number of bee colonies in the New River Valley. We hope to achive this through education of the public, helping new beekeepers, and increasing the amount of our own colonies.

Educate

We are always looking for new ways to educate public on the extreme importance of pollinators to our ecosystem.

Grow

All of our profits, if any, are invested back into growing our apiary. It is a yearly battle with colony collapse a constant threat.

Create

Creating the next generation of beekeepers is extremely important. We stay active in local beekeeping clubs, always happy to help out new members.

Enjoy

It's not all work! We use our own products almost daily, using honey as a substitue for sugar.
